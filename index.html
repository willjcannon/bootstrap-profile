About Me

My name is William Cannon and I'm currently a writer in NYC. I was born and raised in Denver, CO and once I finished college I came to the big apple to make my start as a writer.

I gained a lot of experience as a writer (some good and some bad) but it has been a great learning experience overall. At this point in my career I am looking to branch out and learn new skills that I can add to my tool belt.

Aside from work, I'm a total nerd. I love graphic novels, manga, anime, video games, cosplay, and so much more. My dream job would be to become a video game creator.

So I signed up for the Coding Bootcamp at Columbia and now I'm on my journey to become a coder. Wish me luck!
